What we are trying to make

A short-form learning app that makes scrolling productive.

Doom Scrolling is not trying to remove the habit of scrolling. It redirects that habit toward practical knowledge, skill progress, and creator-led micro-learning.

01

The problem

People already spend hours scrolling short videos, but most feeds do not help them build skills or track what they learned.

02

The solution

Use the same vertical feed behavior, but make every reel useful: AI, cyber, cloud, finance, study, career, coding, and productivity.

03

The learning layer

Each reel can include XP, quizzes, saves, AI summaries, learning streaks, and progress analytics.

04

The business model

Free useful reels create engagement. Premium unlocks mini-courses, expert lessons, downloadable notes, labs, and certificates.

Simple pitch

TikTok for knowledge.

Same addictive scrolling. Better outcome. Users finish a session with XP, saved lessons, summaries, and practical skills instead of only entertainment.
